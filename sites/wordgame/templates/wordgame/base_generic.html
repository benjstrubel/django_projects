How To Use This Crazy Contraption

The category bar at the top shows you what your mystery sentences will be about. The darker the topic label the more the sentence is about that topic.

Think up a funny word for the parts of speech and fill them in.

Click the "Show It" button too see what you created.
Click "Say It" to have the creation read to you in a bad robotic voice. Please be aware that the hamsters powering our servers are elderly so it may take some time for the audio file to be created.

The game tries to learn what topics you like so click the thumbs up or thumbs down icon so we can learn what you like.

When you are ready to play again you can click the "Stored Headline" button to get another game from our database. If you want a current news headline click the "Current Headline" button. If you want try a headline based on your location click the "Local Headline" button.
